Our Story

Maison Cocktail Lisboa was born from a shared passion for the art of cocktails and a desire to create unforgettable experiences.

Created by two friends and bartenders, with a natural connection behind the bar, the project blends French precision with Brazilian energy – all with a taste for elegance and customer experience.

With backgrounds in prestigious venues such as Le Toiny (St. Barth), GIGI Paris and Farol Hotel (Cascais), we decided to bring our shared vision of mixology beyond the traditional bar walls.

Maison Cocktail offers a full-service bar experience:
— Private and corporate cocktail workshops
— Consulting services for hospitality professionals
— Tailor-made events for weddings, parties and team buildings

Whether for an intimate group or a big celebration, our goal is always the same: to offer refined service, spark connection, and turn each bar moment into something special.
