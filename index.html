Our Story

Maison Cocktail Lisboa was born from a shared passion for the art of mixology and the desire to create unforgettable experiences.

Founded by two bartender friends with a natural connection behind the counter, the brand combines French precision with Brazilian energy — refined, attentive, and people-focused.

With experience in iconic places such as Le Toiny (St. Barth), GIGI Paris and the Farol Hotel (Cascais), we wanted to share our vision of cocktails beyond the walls of a classic bar.

Maison Cocktail offers a complete and personalized service:
— Cocktail workshops for individuals and companies
— Hospitality & bar consulting
— Tailor-made events: weddings, parties, team buildings

Whether it's for a small group or a large celebration, our goal remains the same: provide high-quality service, create connection, and turn every bar moment into a unique experience.
